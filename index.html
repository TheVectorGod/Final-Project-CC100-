Welcome to TruthLens

Background

Years have gone by where the Philippines have slowly submerged in different mediums of corruption; affecting those who live a far distance from those in power. Change is temporary, but the effect is vital, here in TruthLens we aspire to shed light upon the issues of corruption from within this nation; big or small and to see everything past our naked eye. Advocating not only pure and unbiased news but also simultaneously promoting change to finally arrive in this long-living country, the people have dealt through too much and it is time for something to happen.

What is Corruption?

Corruption is an act of unethical practices that can be seen in different mediums; most commonly recognized within the political scene. It may involve different methods such as bribery, influence peddling, embezzlement, and fraud. Specifically in the talks of politics, it is referred to when a governmental employee acts in an official capacity for personal gain. It is a completely fraudulent and unethical conduct usually held by those in power.

What We Do

TruthLens is an organization that advocates anti-corruption movements, prioritizing transparency and clarity to all citizens of the Philippines. This website is used to allocate feature articles that relate to the current state and news of the Philippines' concerning issue of corruption as well as addressing different campaigns from within the nation that help in fighting against corruption. We aim to keep this website updated as much as possible, publishing fresh news that speak for our advocacy while also cooperating with others to pan our nation into a better and must-deserved direction.

Vision

Our vision is a nation protected by the forces of corruption and free of fraudulent acts. A country where every citizen cultivates the liberation of transparency and economical security. An office where unethical members of the higher-ups are penalized for their acts of dishonesty and malice. A moment in time where we can all see this nation rise from its roots and collectively move forward together.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver fresh, important, and unbiased news to the public. Collaborating with campaigns who share the same eye and vision, effectively for the purpose of the citizens affected. To attain for change and to avoid malicious ways of using power, keeping an eye out for those who use their power for the wrong reasons, an active and effective form of journalism for the greater good of the people.
